About

Independent systems builder for practical, multi-layer architectures.

Grotaxe works closer to an embedded and infrastructure architect than a conventional portfolio profile. The operating model is simple: build systems that can survive real usage, scale in layers, and stay understandable from hardware edge to interface logic.

The work centers on connected technical ecosystems: SBC and embedded platforms, firmware-adjacent decision making, storage and security concepts, self-hosted services, and control layers that make the whole system usable without diluting its architecture.

Instead of producing disconnected utilities, the focus is on composed environments where device, server, UI, automation, and protocol all reinforce one another. That makes room for Telegram bots as operational surfaces, cryptographic access logic as a product boundary, and private infrastructure as the default substrate rather than an afterthought.

The same approach extends into local AI, memory, and RAG-oriented ideas: not as generic wrappers, but as components in long-term technical systems with clear ownership, offline bias where useful, and a product architecture that can keep evolving over time.
